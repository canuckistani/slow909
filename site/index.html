A new 909 on the block.

We've all heard a million high-bpm loop packs with banging crashes and cymbals, booming kicks and verbed out claps, emulating the classic tunes from artists and innovators like Jeff Mills and Ritchie Hawtin.

This teaser pack of 3 construction kits is a beast of a different flavor entirely - dub tempos and hard swing values from a master at programming resulting in exquisitely recorded stems from a genuine 909. The analog character is all there, captured and processed with high end outboard gear and provided at 24bit / 96khz.
